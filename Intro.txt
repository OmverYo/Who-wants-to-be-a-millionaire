This is Justin Lee's "Who wants to be a millionaire".
You win the game when you reach score of 10.
You are also given 30 coins to try answering the questions.
You will lose if you use up all 30 coins.
Also, you will lose if your answer is incorrect 3 times in a row.
Please type the number of your chosen answer such as -> 3
One simple number will do.
Enjoy!
